# Class sprites — Liberated Pixel Cup (LPC) attribution

The six class character sprites (warrior/rogue/mage/paladin/necromancer/hunter)
in this folder are composited from the **Universal LPC Spritesheet Generator**
(liberatedpixelcup / sanderfrenken), then given a muted-earthy palette pass.

- **License:** CC-BY-SA 3.0 and/or GPL 3.0 (per asset). The exported sheets are
  therefore CC-BY-SA — attribution is REQUIRED in-game and the sheets must be
  shared alike. Full per-asset artist list: `CREDITS.csv` (bundled). License
  text: `LICENSE-LPC.txt`.
- **Generator:** https://github.com/liberatedpixelcup/Universal-LPC-Spritesheet-Character-Generator
- **Layers used:** body + human head + hair/hood, plate & leather armour, robe,
  legs/feet, and weapons (longsword, dagger, mace, magic wand, gnarled staff, bow+quiver).

Attribution must be surfaced in the shipped game's credits screen.
