Substitutions vs. the original effect research (chosen after frame
inspection, visibly better fits):
  - fan_of_knives uses the Pimen Smear VFX 01 frames as a fast spinning
    blade loop ("blade_spin") — Halloween VFX 3 turned out to be a blood
    splash, not a knife.
  - whirlwind uses Pimen Wind 02 "Air Explosion" swirl instead of
    BDragon1727 Free Smoke Fx 2 (the swirl reads much better as a shred).
  - shadowstep / death poofs use Pimen "Smoke Effect 01" (Smoke VFX 1/2)
    instead of Smoke N Dust 01 (not present in the downloads).
